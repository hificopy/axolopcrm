## üèÅ Quick Start (15 Minutes)

### Step 1: Complete Your Profile

1. **Sign In** to your account
2. **Navigate** to Settings ‚Üí Account
3. **Update** your profile information:
   - Full name and title
   - Profile photo
   - Contact information
   - Time zone

### Step 2: Set Up Your Agency

1. **Go to** Settings ‚Üí Organization
2. **Configure** your agency details:
   - Agency name
   - Website domain
   - Logo and branding
   - Business hours

### Step 3: Invite Team Members

1. **Navigate** to Settings ‚Üí Agency
2. **Click** "Invite Team Member"
3. **Enter** team member email
4. **Select** role (Admin or Member)
5. **Send** invitation

### Step 4: Import Your Data

1. **Go to** Contacts ‚Üí Import
2. **Upload** CSV file with contacts
3. **Map** columns to CRM fields
4. **Review** and confirm import

## üë• Understanding User Roles

### Agency Admins

- **Full Access** to all features
- **Can** invite/remove team members
- **Can** manage billing and subscription
- **Can** configure agency settings

### Seated Team Members

- **Read-Only Access** to most features
- **Can** view contacts, leads, and pipeline
- **Cannot** edit, delete, or manage settings
- **Cannot** access billing information

## üìä Core CRM Features

### üéØ Lead Management

#### Adding Leads Manually

1. **Navigate** to Leads page
2. **Click** "Add New Lead"
3. **Fill in** lead information:
   - Name and contact details
   - Company and title
   - Lead source (how they found you)
   - Initial notes
4. **Save** the lead

#### Lead Scoring

Leads are automatically scored based on:

- **Engagement** - Email opens, clicks, website visits
- **Demographics** - Job title, company size, industry
- **Behavior** - Form submissions, content downloads
- **Custom Criteria** - Your specific scoring rules

#### Lead Status Management

- **New** - Recently added leads
- **Contacted** - Initial outreach made
- **Qualified** - Meet your ideal customer criteria
- **Converted** - Became opportunities
- **Archived** - No longer relevant

### üë• Contact Management

#### Contact Organization

- **Tags** - Organize contacts with custom tags
- **Custom Fields** - Add fields specific to your business
- **Relationships** - Link contacts to companies and other contacts
- **Activity Timeline** - View all interactions in one place

#### Contact Import

1. **Prepare** CSV file with columns:
   - First Name, Last Name
   - Email, Phone
   - Company, Title
   - Custom fields
2. **Upload** file to Contacts ‚Üí Import
3. **Map** columns to CRM fields
4. **Review** and confirm

### üíº Opportunity Pipeline

#### Pipeline Stages

Customize your pipeline stages:

1. **Prospecting** - Initial lead qualification
2. **Qualification** - Confirm fit and interest
3. **Proposal** - Send proposal or quote
4. **Negotiation** - Discuss terms and pricing
5. **Closed Won** - Deal successfully closed
6. **Closed Lost** - Deal not successful

#### Managing Opportunities

- **Drag and Drop** - Move deals between stages
- **Value Tracking** - Set deal value and probability
- **Task Creation** - Create follow-up tasks
- **Notes & Activities** - Track all interactions

## üìù Forms & Lead Capture

### Creating Forms

1. **Navigate** to Forms page
2. **Click** "Create New Form"
3. **Choose** form type:
   - Contact Form
   - Lead Capture Form
   - Survey Form
   - Application Form
4. **Build** form using drag-and-drop editor
5. **Configure** settings and notifications
6. **Publish** and embed on your website

### Form Fields Available

- **Text Fields** - Single line, multi-line, rich text
- **Contact Fields** - Email, phone, website
- **Choice Fields** - Dropdown, radio buttons, checkboxes
- **File Upload** - Resume, document uploads
- **Rating Fields** - Star ratings, number scales
- **Date Fields** - Date picker, time picker
- **Conditional Logic** - Show/hide fields based on answers

### Form Embedding

- **Inline Embed** - Full page form
- **Popup Modal** - Triggered by button or timer
- **Slide-in** - Side panel form
- **Standalone Page** - Dedicated landing page

## üìß Email Marketing

### Creating Campaigns

1. **Navigate** to Email Marketing
2. **Click** "Create Campaign"
3. **Select** template or start from scratch
4. **Design** email using drag-and-drop editor
5. **Set up** recipient list
6. **Configure** sending options
7. **Schedule** or send immediately

### Email Templates

- **Pre-built Templates** - Professional designs
- **Custom Templates** - Save your own designs
- **Personalization** - Merge fields for custom content
- **Responsive Design** - Works on all devices

### Campaign Analytics

- **Open Rate** - Percentage of recipients who opened
- **Click-Through Rate** - Percentage who clicked links
- **Conversion Rate** - Percentage who took desired action
- **Unsubscribe Rate** - Percentage who opted out
- **Bounce Rate** - Percentage that couldn't be delivered

## ‚ö° Workflows & Automation

### Creating Workflows

1. **Navigate** to Workflows
2. **Click** "Create Workflow"
3. **Choose** trigger:
   - Form submission
   - Lead created
   - Deal stage changed
   - Email opened/clicked
4. **Add** actions:
   - Send email
   - Create task
   - Update contact
   - Add tag
5. **Set up** conditions and delays
6. **Test** and activate workflow

### Common Workflow Examples

- **Lead Nurturing** - Automatic email sequence for new leads
- **Task Assignment** - Auto-assign leads to team members
- **Follow-up Reminders** - Remind team to follow up
- **Welcome Series** - Onboard new contacts automatically

## üîß Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Import Problems

### Form Issues

- **Not Submitting** - Check required fields
- **Not Receiving Emails** - Verify notification settings
- **Embed Problems** - Test embed code on different browsers
- **Mobile Issues** - Test form on mobile devices

### Email Marketing

- **Low Open Rates** - Check subject lines and sender reputation
- **High Bounce Rates** - Clean email lists regularly
- **Spam Complaints** - Ensure compliance with email laws
- **Delivery Issues** - Verify DNS and authentication settings
