# Axolop CRM - Version 1.0

## Release Overview

Welcome to Axolop CRM Version 1.0 - the first major release of our premium all-in-one CRM for ECOMMERCE, B2B BUSINESS, and REAL ESTATE markets. This release represents a significant milestone in our journey toward building the best CRM on the planet.

### Core CRM Functionality
- **Contact Management**: Comprehensive contact database with search, filtering, and organization capabilities

- **Deal Management**: Opportunity pipeline with stage progression tracking
- **Activity Logging**: Complete activity history for all customer interactions

- **Campaign Creation**: Build and schedule email marketing campaigns

- **Integrated Calendar**: Schedule meetings and appointments directly in the CRM
- **Booking Links**: Create custom booking links for easy scheduling
- **Meeting Management**: Track meeting history and follow-up items

- **Custom Fields**: Add custom data fields to match your business requirements
- **Dashboard Analytics**: Real-time metrics and visualizations for business performance
- **User Preferences**: Personalized settings and experience customization
