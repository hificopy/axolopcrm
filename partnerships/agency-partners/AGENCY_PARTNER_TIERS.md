# Axolop CRM Agency Partner Program
## Referral → Reseller → White-Label Partnership Tiers

---

## Executive Summary

The Agency Partner Program is designed for agencies, consultants, and service providers who want to:
1. **Refer** clients to Axolop and earn commissions
2. **Resell** Axolop under their own pricing to clients
3. **White-label** the entire platform as their own branded solution

Each tier builds on the previous, with increasing revenue potential and customization options.

## Tier 1: Referral Partner

### Overview
The simplest partnership model. Send leads, earn commissions. No inventory, no billing, no support responsibility.

### How It Works
1. Sign up for the Referral Partner Program
2. Get your unique referral link
3. Share with your network
4. Earn 40% recurring commission when they become customers

### Commission Structure
Same as affiliate program:
- **Partner Level (0-4 referrals):** 20% recurring
- **Power Level (5-14 referrals):** 30% recurring
- **Elite Level (15+ referrals):** 40% recurring

### Requirements
- Active Axolop CRM account (any plan)
- Signed Partner Terms of Service
- No monthly fee

### Ideal For
- Consultants who recommend tools
- Business coaches
- Marketing agencies (small)
- Individual influencers

### Application Process
1. Apply at axolop.com/partners
2. Receive approval within 24 hours
3. Access partner dashboard
4. Start referring

## Tier 2: Reseller Partner

### Overview
For agencies who want to include Axolop in their service offerings. You maintain the client relationship, set your own pricing, and earn the margin.

### How It Works
1. Purchase wholesale licenses from Axolop
2. Bundle with your services (e.g., "Done-For-You CRM Setup")
3. Bill clients at your price
4. Keep the difference as margin

### Pricing Structure

**Wholesale Pricing (Your Cost):**

| Plan | Retail Price | Your Wholesale Cost | Minimum Margin |
|------|--------------|---------------------|----------------|
| Sales | $67/mo | $40/mo | $27/mo (40%) |
| Build | $187/mo | $112/mo | $75/mo (40%) |
| Scale | $349/mo | $209/mo | $140/mo (40%) |

**Example Reseller Model:**
- You charge client: $297/mo (your price)
- Your wholesale cost: $112/mo (Build plan)
- Your margin: $185/mo per client

**Volume Discounts (Monthly Minimum):**
| Active Clients | Additional Discount |
|----------------|---------------------|
| 10-24 | 5% off wholesale |
| 25-49 | 10% off wholesale |
| 50-99 | 15% off wholesale |
| 100+ | Custom pricing |

### What You Get
- Wholesale pricing (40%+ margin guaranteed)
- Co-branded client onboarding
- Partner support channel
- Sales enablement materials
- Co-marketing opportunities
- Quarterly partner reviews

### Requirements
- $99/month partner fee
- Minimum 3 client licenses in first 90 days
- 12-month commitment
- Signed Reseller Agreement
- Completed partner certification (2-hour online course)

### Your Responsibilities
- Client acquisition
- First-line support (with escalation to Axolop)
- Client success/retention
- Billing and collections

### Ideal For
- Marketing agencies
- Business consultants
- CRM implementation firms
- Industry-specific service providers

### Application Process
1. Apply at axolop.com/partners/reseller
2. Discovery call with Partner Manager
3. Sign Reseller Agreement
4. Complete certification
5. Get wholesale access
6. Start selling

## Tier 3: White-Label Partner

### Overview
The full white-label solution. Your brand, your domain, your pricing, your support. Axolop becomes invisible to your end clients.

### How It Works
1. We provision a white-labeled instance of Axolop
2. Your branding, your colors, your domain (crm.youragency.com)
3. You sell it as "Your Agency CRM"
4. Clients never see Axolop branding
5. You handle all client interactions

### Pricing Structure

**Platform Fee:**
- $499/month base fee
- Includes: Infrastructure, updates, maintenance, API access

**Per-Seat Pricing:**

| Tier | Seats Included | Per Additional Seat | Your Typical Charge |
|------|----------------|---------------------|---------------------|
| Starter | 50 seats | $12/seat/mo | $50-100/seat/mo |
| Growth | 150 seats | $10/seat/mo | $50-100/seat/mo |
| Scale | 500 seats | $8/seat/mo | $50-100/seat/mo |
| Enterprise | Unlimited | Custom | Custom |

**Example White-Label Economics:**
- Your monthly cost: $499 + (100 seats × $10) = $1,499/mo
- Your revenue at $75/seat: 100 × $75 = $7,500/mo
- Your margin: $6,001/mo (80% margin)

### What You Get

## Partner Comparison Calculator

### Scenario: Agency with 20 Clients

**Option A: Referral Partner**
- 20 clients on Build plan × $74.80 commission = $1,496/mo
- Your effort: Minimal (just referring)
- Client relationship: Axolop owns

**Option B: Reseller Partner**
- 20 clients × $185 margin = $3,700/mo
- Partner fee: -$99/mo
- Net: $3,601/mo
- Your effort: Moderate (support + billing)
- Client relationship: You own

**Option C: White-Label Partner**
- 20 clients × $75/seat charge = $1,500/mo
- Platform fee: -$499/mo
- Seat costs (20 × $12): -$240/mo
- Net: $761/mo
- BUT: You own the brand, the relationship, the future

**When to Choose Each:**
- **Referral:** You want passive income, minimal involvement
- **Reseller:** You want good margins, moderate control
- **White-Label:** You want full control, building a SaaS business
