## Promotional Rules

### What's Allowed:
- Content marketing (blogs, videos, podcasts)
- Email marketing to your own list
- Social media promotion
- Paid advertising (with disclosure)
- Webinars and live demos
- Comparison content (honest reviews)
- Bonus offers (your own bonuses to buyers)

### What's NOT Allowed:
- Bidding on "Axolop" branded keywords in paid ads
- Cookie stuffing or click fraud
- Misleading claims about the product
- Spam of any kind
- Fake reviews or testimonials
- Impersonating Axolop employees
